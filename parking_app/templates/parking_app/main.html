How it Works

  • Sign Up/Log In: Create an account and register your car with your ADMU Car Pass ID.
  • Enter Parking: Use your current location to find the nearest parking zones and see real-time availability.
  • Exit Parking: Log your exit to update slot availability and earn reward points.
  • Dashboard: View historical data and peak hour predictions to plan your parking.

FAQs & Helpdesk

Q: How accurate is the parking availability?

A: Our system updates availability in real-time using data from entry/exit logs. While highly accurate, minor delays might occur.

Q: What if I overstay my parking?

A: Overstaying may result in penalty points on your account. Please check the specific parking rules for each zone.
